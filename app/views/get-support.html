Before reaching out

Before contacting the Cyber Information and Security Division (CISD), you should:

  • review Secure by Design (SbD) guidance from the Government Digital Service (GDS)
  • attend a scheduled Secure by Design briefing session
  • contact your Secure by Design champion

You can also use the SbD Slack channel for support.

Secure by Design sessions

Sessions are being organised with portfolios, who will then notify their teams once scheduled.

The sessions will allow you to:

  • learn what other teams are doing
  • get an insight into a particular aspect of Secure by Design
  • ask your questions

Ask for CISD support

To reach out to CISD for support in implementing Secure by Design principles, complete this contact form.

Projects or services that are currently within scope for Secure by Design will be prioritised for support.
