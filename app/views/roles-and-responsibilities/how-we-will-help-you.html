Our role

The Cyber and Information Security Division (CISD) are responsible for ensuring the cyber security and resilience of DfE's data and technology infrastructure. Cyber security information provided by project or operational teams is a significant contributor towards this.

What support is available to delivery teams

We will be providing services that include:

  • enterprise threat intelligence
  • security architecture guidance
  • threat modelling advice and training
  • cyber security incident management for critical incidents
  • insights into digital services security vulnerabilities via our Continuous Assurance platform
