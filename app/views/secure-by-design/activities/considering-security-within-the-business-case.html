You must include cyber security requirements in your business case. This helps make sure the appropriate funding, resources, skills and time are allocated to manage cyber security risks.

You may need different types of business case as services develop from idea to implementation. These should cover scoping, planning and procurement.

You must include security considerations at every stage, with steps taken to review and refine them as the project matures.

Contact Business Case Hub for practical advice and guidance on business cases.
