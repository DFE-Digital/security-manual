This activity must be performed during the following phases:

Discovery Alpha Private beta

Your team should understand what physical and virtual assets they need to protect.

A detailed list of assets could include:

  • physical devices
  • applications
  • infrastructure
  • data

Your service may need to protect assets that are not listed. You should ensure your service design and workflows account for all digital assets involved.

Your asset documentation should begin during the discovery phase of your project. As the project requirements and service evolves, you should conduct regular reviews of the documented assets. This includes decommissioning any assets that are no longer in use by the service.

For detailed steps, you can check GOV.UK guidance on documenting service assets.

Code repositories

You need to update your code repositories to identify ownership. This supports DfE to assign repositories to teams, service lines and cost centres.

The custom properties added must align with DfE Financial Business Partners Database.

Guidance for adding your custom properties is available for either GitHub or Azure.
