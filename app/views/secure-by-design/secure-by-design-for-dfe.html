The Department for Education (DfE) is implementing Secure by Design across the organisation, seeking to enhance our cyber resilience through the combined cyber security practices from our projects and services.

From 31 January 2025 all projects and services (whether new those undergoing major changes) that go through Cabinet Office spend control process must follow Secure by Design.

If you believe your project or service meets these criteria or have further queries, please contact the Secure by Design team.
